This webpage is an online recipe book. One that contains many recipes from different places around the world. The best recipe book online, one that users around the world create.

How To Use This Online Recipe Book?

You can search your desired recipe by clicking on the "Recipes" button. You can also look at other recipes that users have uploded.

You can also sign up / log in, and share your recipe by uploding it to the website! Just press the "Share Your Recipe" button, and you are good to go! You are more than welcome to grade recipes you've tried so far! the 5 recipes with the highest scores will appear in the top 5 on the "Recipes" page!
